What’s the Game

The game is built around a simple but engaging mechanic:

spin to decide your next move

Each spin unlocks paths, bonuses, or obstacles. Players choose how to continue their journey and which rewards to pursue. Honestly, I've been testing this for weeks and I'm still finding new combinations.
